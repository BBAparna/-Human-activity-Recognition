The Human Activity Recognition database was built from the recordings of 30 study participants performing activities of daily living . The objective of Human activity recognition is the problem of classifying the activities into one of the six activities performed sequences of accelerometer data recorded by specialized harnesses or smart phones into known well-defined movements.¶
